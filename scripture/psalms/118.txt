Blessed are they that search out his witnesses; they shall seek him with their whole heart.
For they that work lawlessness have not walked in his ways.
Thou hast commanded thy commandments to be kept greatly.
Would that my ways be made straight, that I keep thy statutes.
Then shall I not be ashamed, when I look upon all thy commandments.
I shall confess thee with uprightness of heart, when I have learned the judgments of thy righteousness.
I shall keep thy statutes; forsake me not utterly.
Wherewithal shall a young man direct his way? In the keeping of thy words.
With my whole heart have I sought after thee; cast me not away from thy commandments.
In my heart have I hid thy sayings, that I might not sin against thee.
Blessed art thou, O Lord; teach me thy statutes.
With my lips have I made known all the judgments of thy mouth.
I have delighted in the way of thy witnesses, as in all the riches.
In thy commandments shall I reason, and understand thy ways.
In thy statutes shall I meditate; I shall not forget thy words.

My soul hath longed to desire thy judgments at all times.

Behold, I have desired thy commandments; in thy righteousness make me to live.

I spake also of thy witnesses before kings, and I was not ashamed.
And I meditated in thy commandments, which I have greatly loved.
