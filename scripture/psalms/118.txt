Blessed are they that search out his testimonies; they shall seek him with their whole heart.
For they that work iniquity have not walked in his ways.
Thou hast commanded thy commandments be kept exceedingly.
O that my ways may be directed to keep thy statutes.
Then shall I not be ashamed, when I look upon all Thy commandments.
I shall confess thee with uprightness of heart, when I have learned the judgments of thy righteousness.
I shall keep thy statutes; O forsake me not utterly.
Wherewithal shall a young man direct his way? In the keeping of thy words.
With my whole heart have I sought after thee; cast me not away from thy commandments.
In my heart have I hid thy words, that I might not sin against thee.
Blessed art thou, O Lord; teach me thy statutes.
With my lips have I made known all the judgments of thy mouth.
In the way of thy testimonies have I delighted, as in all the riches.
In thy commandments shall I ponder, and consider thy ways.
In thy statutes shall I meditate; I shall not forget thy words.
Recompense thy servant; make me to live, and I shall keep thy words.
Uncover thou my eyes, and I shall consider the wonders out of thy law.
I am a sojourner in the earth; hide not thy commandments from me.
My soul hath longed to desire thy judgments at all times.
Thou hast rebuked the proud; cursed are they that turn aside from thy commandments.

For even princes sat and spake against me, but thy servant pondered in thy statutes.
For even thy testimonies are my meditation, and thy statutes are my counsels.
My soul hath cleaved unto the earth; make me to live according to thy word.
My ways have I made known, and thou hast heard me; teach me thy statutes.

My soul hath slumbered from accidie; strengthen thou me in thy words.
Remove from me the way of iniquity, and have mercy on me with thy law.
I have chosen the way of truth, and thy judgments have I not forgotten.
I have cleaved to Thy testimonies; O Lord, put me not to shame.
I have ran the way of thy commandments, when thou didst enlarged my heart.
Set for me a law, O Lord, the way of thy statutes, and I shall seek it out always.
Make me to understand, and I shall search out thy law; yea, I shall keep it with my whole heart.
Guide me in the path of Thy commandments; for this same I have desired.
Incline my heart unto thy testimonies, and not unto covetousness.
Turn away mine eyes that I behold not vanity; make me to live in thy way.

Behold, I have desired thy commandments; in thy righteousness make me to live.
Let thy mercy come also upon me, O Lord, even thy salvation, according to thy word.
So shall I answer them that reproach me, for I have hoped in thy words.
And take not away utterly from my mouth the word of truth, for I have hoped in thy judgments.
So shall I keep Thy law always, unto the age, and unto the age of the age.
And I walked in widening, for I have sought out thy commandments.
I spake also of Thy testimonies before kings, and I was not ashamed.
And I meditated in Thy commandments, which I have loved exceedingly.
My hands also have I lifted up unto thy commandments, which I have loved, and I pondered in thy statutes.
Remember thy words unto thy servant, of which thou gave me hope.
This hath comforted me in my lowliness, for thy word hath made me to live.

I remembered thy judgments of the age, O Lord, and I was comforted.

All Thy commandments are truth; wrongfully they pursue me, be thou my help.
