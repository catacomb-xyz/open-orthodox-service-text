O give thanks unto the Lord, for he is good, for his mercy is unto the age.
O give thanks unto the God of gods, for his mercy is unto the age.
O give thanks unto the Lord of lords, for his mercy is unto the age.
To him who alone hath done great wonders, for his mercy is unto the age.
To him that made the heavens with understanding, for his mercy is unto the age.
To him that established the earth upon the waters, for his mercy is unto the age.
To him that alone made great lights, for his mercy is unto the age.
The sun for the dominion of the day, for his mercy is unto the age.
The moon and the stars for the dominion of the night, for his mercy is unto the age.
To him that smote Egypt with their firstborn, for his mercy is unto the age.
And brought Israel out from among them, for his mercy is unto the age.
With a strong hand, and with a lofty arm, for his mercy is unto the age.
To him that divided the Red Sea into parts, for his mercy is unto the age.
And led Israel through the midst of it, for his mercy is unto the age.
And shook off Pharaoh and his host into the Red Sea, for his mercy is unto the age.
To him who led his people through the wilderness, for his mercy is unto the age.
To him which smote great kings, for his mercy is unto the age.
And slew mighty kings, for his mercy is unto the age.
Sehon, king of the Amorites, for his mercy is unto the age.
And Og, the king of Bashan, for his mercy is unto the age.
And gave their land for an inheritance, for his mercy is unto the age.
An inheritance unto Israel his servant, for his mercy is unto the age.
For in our humiliation the Lord remembered us, for his mercy is unto the age.
And hath redeemed us from our enemies, for his mercy is unto the age.
Who giveth food to all flesh, for his mercy is unto the age.
O give thanks unto the God of heaven, for his mercy is unto the age.
