O give thanks unto the Lord, that he is good, that unto the age is his mercy.
O give thanks unto the God of gods, that unto the age is his mercy.
O give thanks unto the Lord of lords, that unto the age is his mercy.
To him who alone hath done great wonders, that unto the age is his mercy.
To him that made the heavens with understanding, that unto the age is his mercy.
To him that established the earth upon the waters, that unto the age is his mercy.
To him that alone made great lights, that unto the age is his mercy.
The sun for the dominion of the day, that unto the age is his mercy.
The moon and the stars for the dominion of the night, that unto the age is his mercy.
To him that smote Egypt with their firstborn, that unto the age is his mercy.
And brought Israel out from among them, that unto the age is his mercy.
With a strong hand, and with a lofty arm, that unto the age is his mercy.
To him that divided the Red Sea into parts, that unto the age is his mercy.
And led Israel through the midst of it, that unto the age is his mercy.
And shook off Pharaoh and his host into the Red Sea, that unto the age is his mercy.
To him who led his people through the wilderness, that unto the age is his mercy.
To him which smote great kings, that unto the age is his mercy.
And slew mighty kings, that unto the age is his mercy.
Sehon, king of the Amorites, that unto the age is his mercy.
And Og, the king of Bashan, that unto the age is his mercy.
And gave their land for an inheritance, that unto the age is his mercy.
An inheritance unto Israel his servant, that unto the age is his mercy.
For in our humiliation the Lord remembered us, that unto the age is his mercy.
And hath redeemed us from our enemies, that unto the age is his mercy.
Who giveth food to all flesh, that unto the age is his mercy.
O give thanks unto the God of heaven, that unto the age is his mercy.
