O give thanks unto the Lord, for he is good, for unto the age is his mercy.
O give thanks unto the God of gods, for unto the age is his mercy.
O give thanks unto the Lord of lords, for unto the age is his mercy.
To him who alone hath done great wonders, for unto the age is his mercy.
To him that made the heavens with understanding, for unto the age is his mercy.
To him that established the earth upon the waters, for unto the age is his mercy.
To him that alone made great lights, for unto the age is his mercy.
The sun for the dominion of the day, for unto the age is his mercy.
The moon and the stars for the dominion of the night, for unto the age is his mercy.
To him that smote Egypt with their firstborn, for unto the age is his mercy.
And brought Israel out from among them, for unto the age is his mercy.
With a strong hand, and with a lofty arm, for unto the age is his mercy.
To him that divided the Red Sea into parts, for unto the age is his mercy.
And led Israel through the midst of it, for unto the age is his mercy.
And shook off Pharaoh and his host into the Red Sea, for unto the age is his mercy.
To him who led his people through the wilderness, for unto the age is his mercy.
To him which smote great kings, for unto the age is his mercy.
And slew mighty kings, for unto the age is his mercy.
Sehon, king of the Amorites, for unto the age is his mercy.
And Og, the king of Bashan, for unto the age is his mercy.
And gave their land for an inheritance, for unto the age is his mercy.
An inheritance unto Israel his servant, for unto the age is his mercy.
For in our humiliation the Lord remembered us, for unto the age is his mercy.
And hath redeemed us from our enemies, for unto the age is his mercy.
Who giveth food to all flesh, for unto the age is his mercy.
O give thanks unto the God of heaven, for unto the age is his mercy.
